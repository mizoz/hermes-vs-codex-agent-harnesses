# Repository Instructions

## Authorship

Do not add assistant, model, bot, or generated-by attribution to this repository.
The paper may discuss agent harnesses as the research subject, but metadata,
commits, release notes, and paper bylines must not include AI bylines.

## Public Data Boundary

Use synthetic fixtures or sanitized operational traces only. Do not include live
record-system, message-system, payment, signature-workflow, DNS, private-entity,
secret, or private
business data in public artifacts.

## Evidence Standard

Major factual claims need source support. Prefer primary sources: official docs,
standards, peer-reviewed papers, government reports, and repo-local inspected
code. Mark weak or unverified claims explicitly.
