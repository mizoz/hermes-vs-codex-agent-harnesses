# No-Byline Metadata Policy

The paper and repository may discuss agentic systems as the research subject.
They must not include assistant/model/bot attribution as authorship or
production credit.

Do not add:

- generated-by footer;
- co-authored-by trailer;
- assistant/model byline;
- hidden bot signature;
- release note language implying assistant authorship;
- PR template text attributing the work to an assistant.

Allowed:

- factual discussion of Codex, Hermes, external model sidecars, and agent harnesses as
  technical subjects;
- citations to public product documentation and standards;
- author metadata naming Ahmed or ZalaStack as approved by Ahmed.
